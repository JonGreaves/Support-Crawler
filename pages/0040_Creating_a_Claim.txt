Creating a Claim
Using your internet browser, log in to
Mobius Claims
. For details, see
Logging in to Mobius Claims
. The Dashboard page is displayed.
If you have already logged in, use the direct link in the Top Navigation Bar by clicking the
SEARCH
item. For details on the Top Navigation Bar, see
The Top Navigation Bar
.
The Policy Search enables you to enter search terms by which you expect to find the policy. It also searches for Claims created in
Mobius Claims
.
You  can for example search on
Claim Number
,
Third Party Driver Surname
, or on
Drivers Surname
. You can also search on all of these by entering all information you want to find.
The Claims Cloud searches as you type; you do not have to press Enter or click
SEARCH
. Partial matches are not returned; entering 'smythe' does
not
return matches for 'Smyth' or 'Smythers'. Searches are
not
case sensitive
If your search for policies with claims is successful, then one or more of these may be listed.
If the search criteria do not return any matches, the following messages are displayed:
"No policy results match your search term <SEARCH_TERM>"
"No claim results match your search term <SEARCH_TERM>"
The Action Pane
You may find policies without claims, in which case the Action pane is opened.
Select the
Date
and
Time
of the accident by using the date picker on the left and the time picker on the right. The time can be selected in 15-minute increments or entered manually.
Click the
CREATE CLAIM
button to open the first of the claims panes, the majority of which is Line of Business dependent. See
The Claims Details Page
.
