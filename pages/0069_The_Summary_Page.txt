The Notes Pane
(lower left)
This pane lists the latest added notes.
The
ADD
button enables you to add a note to the claim.
Click
ADD
to open the Add Note popup window. You must select the
Type
of note from the 'Select type' drop-down list before submitting the new note. Enter the contents of the note in the text box below. Click
OK
to save any changes. Alternatively click the
Close
button in the top right of the window to discard the new note.
The
VIEW ALL
button shows the number of notes for the selected Claim in brackets .
Click
VIEW ALL
to open the
View Notes
popup window. Use the scroll bar on the right to view all notes if necessary. To close the popup window, click the
Clos
e
button in the top right of the window.

Financial Summary Pane
(middle)
The following columns are displayed:
Claim Head
- Default setting is 'Overall'
Click the expand button
on the right to display detail headings such as 'Accidental Damage'. The rows listed represent the selected sections when adding a reserve. See also
The Financials Tab
. Click the expand
button on the right of a heading to view the affected parties. Click the collapse
button to close the various detail lines.
Outstanding
- amount that remains to be paid out
Paid
- amount paid out to date
Authorised
- amount authorised to date
Gross Incurred
- amount of claims incurred before deducting recoveries
Recovery Reserve
- amount held in reserve for recovery
Recovered Amount
- amount recovered
Net Incurred
- amount of claims incurred after deducting recoveries
Arrow-Down
button - Click this button on the right to expand summary data.
Arrow-Up
button - Click this  button on the right to collapse summary data.
A tick mark
on the left of the Claim Head column indicates that the item has been settled.
Excesses Pane
(middle)
The following columns are displayed:
Excess Head
- Default setting is 'Overall'
Click the expand button
on the right to display detail headings such as 'Accidental Damage'. Click the expand
button on the right of a heading to view the
voluntary
and
compulsory
excess amount per policy. Click the collapse
button to close the various detail lines.
Amount
- the excess amount stated in the relevant policy
Deducted
- the excess amount deducted on the basis of this claim
Claim Ownership Pane
(middle)
The Claim Handler pane displays the Insurer / Reinsurer name and has a drop-down list, enabling you to see the current claim handler.
To select a different
Claim Handler
, open the drop-down list, select the appropriate handler, and then click the
TRANSFER
button. The following message is briefly displayed in the lower right of the page:
To assign the claim to yourself, click the
ASSIGN TO ME
button.
The following message is briefly displayed in the lower right of the page
The
TRANSFER OWNERSHIP
and
TAKE OWNERSHIP
buttons change the Agent ownership for the selected claim.
To transfer ownership
to
another agent, click TRANSFER OWNERSHIP, select the 'new' agent from the
Select agent
drop-down list, and then click TRANSFER
To transfer ownership
from
another agent, click TAKE OWNERSHIP, select the 'new' agent from the
Select agent
drop-down list, and then click TRANSFER
Outstanding Diaries Pane
(middle)
To add a new entry, click the
ADD DIARY
button on the top left. This opens the Add Diary Entry page. See
Add Diary Entry Page
.
Existing Diary entries are shown in the tabs below:
The Outstanding Diaries pane has the following tabs. The number in the tab label represents the number of outstanding tasks under that tab.
Outstanding
Completed
The columns are the same for both tabs:
Claim Number
Diary Type
Reminder Date
(includes date and time)
Due Date
(includes date and time)
Click the
VIEW
button for a given claim to inspect diary entry details. See
View Diary Entry Page
.
You now have the following options:
Click
DIARY ON
to select a time-period by which to move the Diary entry and save it.
Click
SAVE
to secure any changes.
Click
ACTION
to complete the Diary task.
Click
CANCEL
to discard any changes.
You are returned to the previous page after any of the above actions
