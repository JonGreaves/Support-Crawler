The Payments Page
The Payments page enables you to access all payment related information.
Using your internet browser, log in to
Mobius Claims
. For details, see
Logging in to Mobius Claims
. The
Dashboard
page is displayed.
Select PAYMENTS from the Top Navigation Bar. This opens the Payments page.
For details on the Top Navigation Bar, see
The Top Navigation Bar
.
Claim Status Selection
To filter the payment status for the items listed in the table below:
Select a given
Pending level
from the
Select status
drop-down list. You must click
OK
to apply the filter.
To reset the filtered list, open the drop-down list and then click
Select status
.
The Claim Status is shown in the
Status
column.
