Vehicle Search Results screen
This screen displays the search results returned after a search is performed on the make of vehicle in the Vehicle Details screen (by clicking the search icon
next to the
Make
field).  See
3: Vehicle Information Screen
for more details on working in that screen.
The results returned in this screen reflect the contents of the ABI vehicle file as filtered by the details you have already entered in the Vehicle Details screen. Vehicle details which are used as filters are the fields from the
Make
and
Model
up to
Gearbox
, as laid out in the Vehicle Details screen.  You can select the appropriate vehicle simply by highlighting it and clicking
Select
. This copies the details for the selected vehicle into the Vehicle Details screen and includes the ABI vehicle code. This code is used by the scheme to rate the vehicle for insurance.
