Pending Confirmation
A 'Pending Confirmation' reserve is a reserve which has been added with a higher amount then the user's reserve limit.
If you confirm a reserve in excess of your limits
the status of your entry will be marked 'Pending Confirmation', and  a
CONFIRM
button is added to your entry.
To proceed, you have the following options:
Ask a member of staff with the appropriate reserve limit
and
the "Confirm Reserve" permission to confirms the pending reserve. For details on Confirm Reserve permissions, see
The Tasks Tab
.
Request to have your own reserve limit raised sufficiently
For details on reserve limits, see
The Authority Reserve Groups Page
.

Authorising Payments
Each level of authorisation needs a new user to authorise. This means that a given payment would be added by
User X
, pre-authorised by
User Y
, and authorised by
User Z
.
To pre-authorise payments, click
PRE-AUTH
on the right.
To authorise pre-authorised payments, click
AUTH
on the right.
Note: You can only click
CANCEL CHEQUE
for cheque  payments that have been authorised
and
for which none of the payment has been recovered.
Removing Payments
Click the
REMOVE
button to remove Payments which have not been pre-authorised. This opens a
Payment Confirmation
window.
Are you sure you wish to remove this payment?
- select a reason from the 'Select Reason' drop-down list. This is mandatory. This list is configurable through the Configurable Lists page. See
The Configurable Lists Page
.
Additional Notes
- Add further details regarding the payment removal. This is mandatory if you have selected
Other
from the 'Select Reason' drop-down list above.
Click
YES
to remove the payment. Alternatively, click
NO
to keep the payment in place.
Note:
Pre-authorised payments cannot be removed
You may not have sufficient rights to (pre-)authorise any payments
Removed payments are not actually removed from the Payments pane but the
Status
field is set to 'Removed' instead.
