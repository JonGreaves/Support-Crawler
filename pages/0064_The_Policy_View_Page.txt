The Policy View page
This page displays any configured information (configured in the CSV files, like the FNOL fields) in collapsible panels and nothing is editable. The purpose of the
Policy View
page is to display the configured policy information using the history of the policy at the date and time of the incident (as captured in the
Incident Date
question during FNOL).
Example:
If the Incident Date question is captured as
11/06/2019
, and the policy has an MTA on
18/06/2019
, then the MTA information is not displayed because it had not been applied to the policy at the date and time of the incident. This also applies to any configured FNOL fields if these have been pre-populated from
Mobius Claims
.

The Claims Details Pane
(middle left)
Select any of the red or green links to open the Claims Details page.
This pane is Line of Business dependent.
Click
EXISTING CLAIMS
to access existing claims,  see
Policy Search Results
.
The Notes Pane
(lower left)
This pane lists the latest added notes. The
ADD
button enables you to add a note to the claim.  The
VIEW ALL
button shows the number of notes for the selected Claim in brackets.
Click
ADD
to open the Add Note popup window. You must select the
Type
of note from the 'Select type' drop-down list before submitting the new note. Enter the contents of the note in the text box below. Click
OK
to save any changes. Alternatively click the Close
button in the top right of the window to discard the new note.
Click
VIEW ALL
to open the View Notes popup window. Use the scroll bar on the right to view all notes if necessary. To close the popup window, click the
Close
button in the top right of the window.
The Claim Number Pane
(upper right)
This pane shows the Claim Number, the status ('Open' for example), and who the claim is currently assigned to.

The Summary tab
When you select the Summary tab on the
Policy View
page, all panes on this tab are automatically expanded. Click any blue bar to toggle between collapsing and expanding the relevant pane. All fields are informational only. There is no option to dig down on fields.
Note that some content used in the above example is LOB specific.

The Documents tab
Click any of the light blue bars to toggle between collapsing and expanding the relevant pane  per policy history. For example, from 'Start Date' to 'First MTA', and then from 'MTA' to 'End Date'.
The
Preview

The Correspondence Tab

PREVIEW

The Financials Tab
The financials tab enables you to view all
Open Transactions
and all
Settled Transactions
for  the selected policy.
All fields are informational only. There is no option to dig down on fields.
The Loss Ratio Tab
The Loss Ratio tab enables you to view the loss
Ratio
percentage on the basis of the
Policy Premium
paid set of against the
Claim Value
.
Click the down-arrow
q
at the end of the first line to view the ratio per year.
Click the down-arrow
q
at the end of the first line to view the ratio per claim for the selected year.
All fields are informational only.
Click the up-arrow
p
at the end of the line to collapse an expanded line.
