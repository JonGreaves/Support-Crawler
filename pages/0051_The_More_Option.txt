The 'More'
drop-down list is part of the
the
Incident Actions
pane
on the
Summary
page. For details, see
The Incident Actions Pane (middle)
.
The purpose of the
drop-down list is to provide the following additional options:
Reallocate Claim - see
Reallocate Claim

MIAFTR
Reallocate Claim
To reallocate a claim, select
Reallocate claim
from the 'More'
drop-down list on the
the I
ncident Actions
pane
on the Summary page. This opens the
Search for a policy
window.
Enter the exact policy number to which you want to reallocate the claim and then click
SEARCH
.
Click the policy listed in the 'Search for a policy' window
to proceed.
Note: You can only reallocate claims that have not yet been allocated a reserve.
