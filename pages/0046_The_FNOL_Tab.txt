Fields
All fields marked with an asterisk (*) must be completed.
Who has the claim been notified by?
* - Select the party from the drop-down list.
How was the claim notified?
* - Select the means of communication from the drop-down list.
Date claim notified
- Enter the date or use the date picker. See

.

* - Select the vehicle from the drop-down list.

Type of claim
* - Select the type of claim from the drop-down list.
Insurer's reference
- Enter a reference for this claim.
