Logging Out of
Mobius Claims
You can log out from
Mobius Claims
anywhere in the application.
Click the
Hi <Your_First_Name>
drop-down list  in the top right of the page.
Select
Logout
. This opens the 'Mobius Login Server - Logout' page.
Click
'here'
to log back in. This takes you to the 'Dashboard' page.
To log in from the 'Dashboard' page, see:
Logging in to Mobius Claims
.
Inactivity Timeout
After a period of inactivity you may be automatically logged out from your current session.
If you have administrator rights, you can change the general
inactivity timeout period.
Note:
To change the Screen Timeout (this applies to all users):
Select
Configuration
,
PARAMETERS
.
Select
Miscellaneous
G
roup
from the
Group
Filter drop-down list (optional).
Select
INT
(eger) from the
Type
Filter drop-down list (optional).
Locate the
Screen
Time Out
setting.
Click the
EDIT
button on the right, and then enter the time out in minutes. To disable screen time out, enter '-1'.
Click
SAVE
to apply the new time out, or click
CANCEL
to discard any changes.
Press
F5
to refresh your screen.
For more details, see
The Parameters Page
.
When a Session Timeout Warning is displayed, click
Renew Session
to return to the most recent page used.
