Vehicle Summary screen
Because there may be more than one vehicle on a policy, you will first need to specify a vehicle when you edit details such as modifications. The Vehicle Summary screen is used to select which vehicle details are to be edited.
The Vehicle Summary screen also allows additional vehicles to be added or the details of existing vehicles to be removed.
