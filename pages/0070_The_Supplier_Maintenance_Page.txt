Using your internet browser, log in to
Mobius Claims
. For details, see
Logging in to Mobius Claims
. The Dashboard page is displayed.
If you have already logged in, but are no longer on the Administration page, use the direct link in the Top Navigation Bar by clicking the
ADMINISTRATION
item on the left. For details on the Top Navigation Bar, see
The Top Navigation Bar
.
The Administration page provides a central location to access software related settings.
Click
SUPPLIER MAINTENANCE
to open the
Supplier Maintenance
page.
All suppliers used can be maintained from this page.
The
Supplier Type
drop-down list enables you to filter the type of supplier you want to list in the table below. The
SHOW ALL
button returns you from the filtered list to the full list of suppliers. To maintain supplier types, select and edit the
Supplier Types
item from the drop-down list on the Configurable Lists page. See
The Configurable Lists Page
Columns
The Supplier Maintenance table has the following columns:
Name
- the supplier's business name
Address
- the supplier's business address
Postcode
- the supplier's business postcode
Email
- the supplier's business email address
Supplier Type
- the kind of business the supplier is in
To
ADD
a new Diary Type, see
Adding a Supplier
To
REMOVE
a Diary Type, see
Removing a Supplier
To
EDIT
or view a Diary Type, see
Editing a supplier
Note: To return from one of the option pages to the Administration page, select the
ADMINISTRATION
option from the Top Navigation Bar.
Adding a Supplier
Click the
ADD
button to open the Add supplier page.
Fields
The Add supplier page has the following fields:
Supplier Type
- select the appropriate type from the drop-down list (mandatory)
Section
- tick one or more check boxes:
Accidental Damage
Personal Injury
Third Party Damage
Name
- business name of the supplier (required)
Address Line 1
- business address of the supplier (required)
Address Line 2
- business address of the supplier
Address Line 3
- business address of the supplier
Address Line 4
- business address of the supplier
Address Line 5
- business address of the supplier
Postcode
- postcode for the business address (required)
Email
- supplier's email address
Payment Details
(bank details for the supplier)
Account Holder Name
- as stated on their bank account
Account Number
- move cursor to the left before entering, validation takes place on the number of digits entered.
Sort Code
- move cursor to the left before entering, validation takes place on the number of digits entered.
Preferred Payment Method
- select the preferred payment method from the drop-down list (Bacs Payment, Cheque, Direct from Insurer)
VAT Registered?
- tick the checkbox if the supplies is VAT Registered
Nominal Code
- enter the applicable financial account code
You now have the following options:
Click
SAVE
to secure your input. The following message is briefly displayed on the bottom right of the page: 'Item saved'.
Click
CANCEL
to discard the new Supplier details.
Removing a Supplier
Click the
REMOVE
button for the Supplier you want to delete. The following message is briefly displayed on the bottom right of the page: 'Supplier removed'.
No confirmation will be asked
The removal of a Supplier cannot be undone
You will no longer be able to edit Supplier details for claims that are still using the removed supplier
Editing a supplier
Click the
EDIT
button for the Supplier you want to amend.
Amend the supplier details fields as required.
Note:
Any changes made will affect all claims to which the selected Supplier has been added.
You now have the following options:
Click
SAVE
to secure your changes. The following message is briefly displayed on the bottom right of the page: 'Data saved'.
Click
CANCEL
to discard the amended Supplier details.
