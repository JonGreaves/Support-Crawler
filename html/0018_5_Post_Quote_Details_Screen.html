5: Post Quote Details Screen

This screen is displayed when you convert a prospect to a policy. Enter any details that are required for the policy documentation but do not impact on the quoted premium.

Information entered here will be displayed in the Summary Screen or (one of the screens accessible from the Summary Screen) after the policy has been converted. See 4: 4: Summary Screen for more details on accessing this information.

As usual, any mandatory fields are highlighted in a different colour. Some fields may already be populated if the relevant questions were completed during data capture.

  • When converting to policy, a number of further screens can be displayed. These can be customised to meet an individual company's requirements and so it is not possible to give a definitive list. The screens which are displayed in these circumstances could be selected from a variety of different screen types (see over page):

  • message boxes containing reminders of post quote activities

  • checklists of required documents or activities

  • questions about further sales

  • questions about how renewals should be handled

  • reminders about any required questions

  • reminders about required forms of words (such as an FCA declaration or a sales status screen)
