The Payments Page

The Payments page enables you to access all payment related information.

Using your internet browser, log in to Mobius Claims. For details, see Logging in to Mobius Claims. The Dashboard page is displayed.

Select PAYMENTS from the Top Navigation Bar. This opens the Payments page.
For details on the Top Navigation Bar, see The Top Navigation Bar.

Claim Status Selection

To filter the payment status for the items listed in the table below:

Select a given Pending level from the Select status drop-down list. You must click OK to apply the filter.

To reset the filtered list, open the drop-down list and then click Select status.

The Claim Status is shown in the Status column.

Columns

Tick box - Tick this box to select all payments, tick the box of any given payment to select that payment. Untick all or any boxes to deselect payments.

  • Claim Number - Allocated Claim number

  • Date Created - Claim creation date

  • Status - Claim Pending level

  • Level 1 - Allocated member of staff for this level

  • Level 2 - Allocated member of staff for this level

  • Level 3 - Allocated member of staff for this level

  • Payee - Claim Payee

  • View - See Buttons

  • Amount - Claim amount

  • Claim Info - Claim details, use the Chevron buttons to open and close the drop-down lists containing the Driver, Passengers and Third-Party (TP) Drivers.

Buttons

  • DOCUMENTS - provides access to any claim related documents.
    Click this button to open and close the document drop-down list.

To open a given document, click the document name. This opens a new browser tab.

To close a given document, click the Windows 'Close' button(X) on the tab label.
Do not click the Windows 'Close' button on the top right.

  • PAYMENT - provides payment details.
    Click this button to open the Payment Details page. See .
