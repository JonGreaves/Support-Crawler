The Close / Reopen Tab

The CLOSE / REOPEN tab is part of the the Incident Actions menu on the Summary page.

Note: This tab is named REOPEN when the claim has been closed. Its function is to closing or reopening claims.

For details on the REOPENtab, see The REOPEN tab

For details on the CLOSEtab, see The CLOSE tab

For details on the Summary page, see The Summary Page.

The REOPEN tab

Click the REOPEN tab to reopen the Claim, and then select a reason from the drop-down list .

Reopened Reasons are is configurable. See The Configurable Lists Page.

Click YES to reopen the claim. This will take you back to the Policy Search page. See The Search Page. The Claim Status is changed to 'Reopened'. You can verify this in the Most Recent Claims pane on the Dashboard page. See 2: Using Mobius Claims.

Click NO to leave the claim closed.

The CLOSE tab

Click the CLOSE tab to close the Claim, and then select a reason from the drop-down list.

Close Claim Reasons are is configurable. See The Configurable Lists Page.

Click YES to close the claim. This will take you back to the Policy Search page. See The Search Page. The Claim Status is changed to 'Closed'. You can verify this in the Most Recent Claims pane on the Dashboard page. See 2: Using Mobius Claims.

Click NO to leave the claim open.
