The Top Navigation Bar

Using your internet browser, log in to Mobius Claims. For details, see Logging in to Mobius Claims. The Dashboard page is displayed by default after logging in.

The Top Navigation Bar is displayed throughout Mobius Claims, with the exception of the About option, and provides direct access to the following items:

  • CLAIMS CLOUD - click this banner to open the Dashboard page.
    The banner name may be replaced with your company banner / logo.
    See also 2: Using Mobius Claims.

  • SEARCH - click to open the Policy Search drop-down list.
    See The Search Page.

  • DIARY - click to open the Diary Search page.
    See The Diary Page .

  • PAYMENTS - click to open the Payments page.
    See The Payments Page.

  • ADMINISTRATION - click to open the Administration page
    See 3: The Administration Page.

  • CONFIGURATION - click to open the Configuration page
    See 4: The Configuration Page.

Note: With the exception of the 'THE CLAIMS CLOUD' banner and About, clicking any of the Top Navigation Bar options when you are not logged in, also take you to the login page.

See also Logging in to Mobius Claims and About.
